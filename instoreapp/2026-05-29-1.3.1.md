# InStore App 1.3.1
[![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/milestone-v1.3.1-green?logo=github)](https://github.com/fiskaltrust/fiskaltrust-instore-app/milestones?state=closed)

<!--truncate-->

## HomeScreen WebView configuration

The HomeScreen WebView can now be enabled and its URL configured per device directly in the local settings.
When active, the blue header bar is hidden, letting the WebView run in true fullscreen.
Settings and receipt history remain just a left-edge swipe away.

![webview_homescreen](images/v1.3.1_webview_homescreen.png)

_Affected issue(s):_ [#706](https://github.com/fiskaltrust/fiskaltrust-instore-app/issues/706), [#594](https://github.com/fiskaltrust/fiskaltrust-instore-app/issues/594)

---

## Settings access PIN

The settings page can now be locked with a 6-digit PIN, keeping unauthorized users like waiters from making configuration changes. Access is granted only after the correct PIN is entered, with a limit of 5 attempts per hour to deter brute-force attacks.

![settings_pin](images/v1.3.1_settings_pin.png)

_Affected issue(s):_ [#699](https://github.com/fiskaltrust/fiskaltrust-instore-app/issues/699)

---

## UI/UX design refresh

Continuation of the UI overhaul started in 1.3.0. The app's look and feel has been aligned with the new fiskaltrust design system, including a redesigned email and SMS input popups on the digital receipt screen. A new "Thank you" screen now closes the receipt flow, replacing the previous "Receipt was accepted..." notification which could appear on the wrong device in multi-terminal setups.

![ui_design_refresh](images/v1.3.1_ui_design_refresh.png)
