# InStore App 1.2.8
 [![Static Badge](https://img.shields.io/badge/milestone-v1.2.8-green?logo=github)](https://github.com/fiskaltrust/fiskaltrust-instore-app/milestone/4?closed=1) <!-- markdown-link-check-disable-line -->


<!--truncate-->

## Landscape display support

The InStore App now provides basic landscape display support, allowing you to use full functionality on devices in landscape orientation. The app remains optimized for portrait mode but will adapt to landscape when needed.

_Affected issue:_  
[#356](https://github.com/fiskaltrust/fiskaltrust-instore-app/issues/356) <!-- markdown-link-check-disable-line -->

---

## Receipt preview: head down rotation support

Receipt preview now supports head down rotation, making it easier to show receipts to customers when the device is rotated.

_Affected issue:_  
[#304](https://github.com/fiskaltrust/fiskaltrust-instore-app/issues/304) <!-- markdown-link-check-disable-line -->

---

## Payment in progress screen

A new status screen shows information about ongoing payment transactions, providing better feedback during payment processing instead of returning to the Home screen.

_Affected issue:_  
[#226](https://github.com/fiskaltrust/fiskaltrust-instore-app/issues/226) <!-- markdown-link-check-disable-line -->
