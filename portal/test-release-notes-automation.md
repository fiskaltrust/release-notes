## Summary
We’ve upgraded the editing process for PosOperator invitations to a new React form, making it easier and faster for you to manage your invitations with inline validation.

## Why This Matters
This improvement allows PosDealers to quickly review and adjust invitation details without the hassle of full page reloads. You can now make corrections on the spot, which saves time and reduces frustration.

## What Changed
- The editing of PosOperator invitations now uses a modern React form.
- Inline validation helps you catch errors immediately as you edit.
- The overall experience is more consistent with the invitation creation process.
- Updates are processed faster, enhancing your workflow.

## Impact
All PosDealers will benefit from this update. You don’t need to take any action; just start using the new editing feature as it becomes available. Screenshots showcasing the new interface are included for your reference.
